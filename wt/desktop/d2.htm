Delivery Details

And How is delivery time determined?

Sellers procure and ship the items within the time specified on the item details page. Some items have to be imported from outside India. These items are sometimes mentioned as "Imported" on the item details page and can take 10 or more days to reach you. Business days exclude public holidays and Sundays.Delivery times for individual sellers will be available along with other details.

When is an item Preorder or Forthcoming?Such items are soon to be released and can be prebooked for you. The item will be shipped to you on the day of it's official release launch and will reach you in 2 to 4 business days. The Preorder duration varies from item to item. Once known, release time and date is mentioned. Eg: 5th May, August 3rd week.

What does ‘Back In Stock Soon’ for a product mean?What does ‘Back In Stock Soon’ for a product mean?
The item is popular and has been sold out. You can however ‘book’ an order for the product and it will be shipped according to the timelines mentioned by the seller.

When is a product Temporarily Unavailable?When is a product Temporarily Unavailable?
The product is currently out of stock and is not available for purchase. The product could to be in stock soon. Use the ‘Notify Me’ feature to know when it is available for purchase.

When is an item Out of Stock?When is an item Out of Stock?
Currently, the item is not available for sale. Use the 'Notify Me' feature to know once it is available for purchase.

When is an item Permanently Discontinued?When is an item Permanently Discontinued?
This product is no longer available because it is obsolete and/or production for it has been discontinued.

When is a book Out of Print?When is a book Out of Print?
This product is not available because it is no longer being published and has been permanently discontinued.

How is the Order Confirmation done?How is the Order Confirmation done?
With every order, you will receive an email containing the details of the order placed by you. 
You will be notified when the seller receives your order and confirms it. You will also be notified when the seller ships the item(s) to you.
Shipping details will be provided with the respective tracking number(s).

How are the items delivered?How are the items delivered?
Sellers process shipments through select couriers, depending on the items ordered and location.
